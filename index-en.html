FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to pay monthly fees after the website is built?+
No. You pay once and the website is yours forever. The only ongoing cost is hosting at $50/month, which keeps your site live, fast, and maintained. No annual contracts, no hidden fees.
How long does it take to build my website?+
Standard delivery is 10 business days. Premium packages are delivered in 14 days. We back this with a guarantee: if we miss the deadline, you get $200 off your total.
What if I don't have photos or written content?+
We write all the copy for your site. For photos, we use professional stock images while you gather real ones from your business. We swap them out for free whenever you're ready.
Will my website actually show up on Google?+
Yes. Every site includes local SEO: title tags, meta descriptions, city-level keywords, and full Google Business Profile setup. Most clients see their first Google traffic within 2 to 4 weeks of launch.
What industries do you work with?+
We specialize in local service businesses: plumbers, electricians, roofers, HVAC, painters, landscapers, salons, barbershops, auto repair, dentists, event rentals, restaurants, and more. If you serve local customers, we can build a site that gets you found.
What if I'm not happy with the result?+
You review the site before it ever goes live. If you're not 100% satisfied, we keep revising until you are. If we genuinely can't make you happy before launch, you get a full refund. Zero risk on your end.
