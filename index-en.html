Frequently Asked Questions

Questions about Kalnyesgrowth

Do I need to pay monthly fees after my website is built? +
No. You pay once and the website is yours forever. The only recurring cost is hosting ($50/month), which covers keeping your site live, fast, and maintained. No annual contracts, no surprises.
How long does it take to build my website? +
Our standard delivery is 10 business days. Premium packages are delivered in 14 days. We guarantee these timelines: if we miss them, you get $200 off your total.
What if I don't have photos or written content? +
We handle the copywriting for your site. For photos, we use professional stock images while you gather real ones from your business. We update with your actual photos at no extra cost, anytime.
Will my website show up on Google? +
Yes. Every site includes local SEO: title tags, meta descriptions, city-level keyword targeting, and Google Business Profile optimization. Most clients see their first Google traffic within 2 to 4 weeks of launch.
What industries do you work with? +
We specialize in local service businesses: plumbers, electricians, roofers, HVAC, painters, landscapers, salons, barbershops, auto repair, dentists, event rental companies, restaurants, and more. If you serve customers locally, we can build a site that gets you found.
What if I'm not happy with the result? +
You review your site before it ever goes live. If you're not 100% satisfied, we keep revising until you are. And if we can't make you happy before launch, we refund you in full. Zero risk.
